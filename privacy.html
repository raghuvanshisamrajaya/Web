Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [09-06-2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Raghuvanshi Health Care. We are dedicated to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our online healthcare and e-commerce services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification: Full name, email address, phone number, shipping/billing address, age, and gender.
  • Health-Related Data: Basic health data if necessary for consultations or product suitability.
  • Payment Data: Secured payment information (via payment gateway, not stored by us).
  • Technical & Device Info: IP address, browser type, operating system, access time, referral URLs, etc.
  • Account Details: Login credentials, order history, saved preferences, and communication records.

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your data for purposes such as:

  • Fulfilling and managing orders, consultations, and home services
  • Processing payments and refunds securely
  • Sending order status, shipping updates, and support communication
  • Health advice (if opted-in or during services)
  • Marketing (with user consent only)
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Legal obligations and fraud prevention

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted third parties such as:

  • Payment processors and logistics partners
  • Licensed healthcare providers (if relevant)
  • IT and cloud service providers
  • Regulatory authorities when legally mandated

All third parties are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality and use your data only for the intended purpose.

5. Consent

By using our website, creating an account, or making a purchase, you agree to the collection and use of your personal data as outlined in this policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law (e.g., tax, accounting, or legal obligations).

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may impact website functionality.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw marketing consent
  • Complain to a data protection authority (if applicable)

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices such as HTTPS encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your data. However, no system is entirely immune from cyber threats.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without verified parental consent.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such external sites. We encourage you to read their policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Effective Date." We recommend checking this page regularly.
