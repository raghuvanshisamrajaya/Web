Cookies Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2025

At Raghuvanshi Health Care, we value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the technologies we use. This Cookies Policy explains how and why cookies may be stored on and accessed from your device when you use or visit our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device to help websites function properly and provide analytics, personalization, and marketing. These files do not access personal information from your device or install malware.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for our website to function (e.g., cart management, login authentication).
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about how users interact with the site for improvement purposes.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences like language, region, and display settings.
  • Marketing Cookies: Help deliver relevant advertising and track ad performance (used only with consent).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure website functionality and security.
  • Remember your shopping preferences and login status.
  • Improve user experience and website performance.
  • Analyze traffic and user interactions using tools like Google Analytics.

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services such as payment gateways, analytics tools, and ad networks to place cookies on your device. These cookies are governed by the respective third-party privacy policies.

Your Cookie Choices

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies. You can modify your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings:

  • Delete existing cookies.
  • Block new cookies.
  • Receive notifications before cookies are stored.

Note: Disabling cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

Managing Cookies
